What is HMAM?

The Hierarchical Motivation Appraisal Model (HMAM) is a multi-layer filter your brain uses—often subconsciously—to decide whether to take action. It rapidly weighs Reward, Effort, Risk, Past Results, and Belief Biases, all modulated by your dopamine and energy levels. If the equation checks out, you feel real drive; if not, you stall, procrastinate, or feel stuck.

HMAM isn’t just theory: it’s drawn from over 50 peer-reviewed studies, blends evolutionary psychology, modern neuroscience, and behavioral economics, and is currently under peer review.
